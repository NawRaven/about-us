Luna pioneers' Greeting

Why we create this project?

Well, as you visited our website, you will know this project is about a fantisy movie,Harry potter. We created this project to share our love for the Harry Potter series and to provide a platform for Myanmar fans to connect and discuss their favorite moments, characters, and theories. And we hope this will ease our mind and give us a break from our daily life.Expecially for the students who are studying hard on computer science and web development. We believe that this project will not only be a fun and engaging experience for us but also for the fans of Harry Potter in Myanmar.

With the first step of us,with this project we expect the experiences and new skills about web development. In the future,we hope to create more websites and application that will be helpful for the people in Myanmar. And we hope to be the pioneers of web development in Myanmar.
